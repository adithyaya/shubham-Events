Bringing Every Celebration to Life

We work in flowers by the thousand, in colours that spill into the street, and in traditions that have been honoured for generations.

A haldi wants sunlight and turmeric yellow. A griha pravesh wants the gate dressed so the whole street knows. A puja wants brass, jasmine and stillness. A birthday wants colour, laughter and life.

We handle the arch over the gate, the rangoli at the threshold, the garlands, the seating, the lighting after dark — so that on the day itself, your only job is to be there for it.
